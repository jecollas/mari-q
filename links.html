Random D&D Character Generator: This is a random generator that I made in my sophomore year of college for a programming class project. The purpose of this generator is to create a new Player Character for the tabletop roleplaying game, Dungeons and Dragons. Features of this generator include:

  • An accurate stat portrayal that organizes a character's with the class selection in mind (example: a bard would have Charisma as their highest stat).
  • A complete listing of all deities, races, subraces, classes, and backgrounds in the D&D 5e Player's Handbook and all other official Wizard's of the Coast material.
  • Multiple gender options that allow for the random character to be trans, nonbinary, or intersex.

Ariel Joy: A random generator that I created for a friend of mine in an attempt to spread a little bit more love and positivity.

Marvelverse: Art block is a common problem amongst my group of friends, so I wanted to build a series of random generators that would supply them with ideas of what to draw. Web design by myself, slogo and banner design by Katie Thornton. This site includes:

  • Four random generators for art and writing prompts.
  • Character reference pages for all major characters.
  • A gallery for art dedicated to characters in the server.
  • A gallery for art dedicated to characters in the D&D campaign currently run by members of the server.
